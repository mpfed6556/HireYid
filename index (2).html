Privacy & Security

HireYid — Our commitment to your data
🔒 HireYid handles sensitive personal information — resumes, ID selfies, contact details. Protecting it is our top priority. This page describes how access is designed and enforced.

Who can see uploads & applications

  • The recruiter you applied to — and only that recruiter's team — can view the resume, photo and details you submit to their position. Applying to one recruiter never exposes your information to any other.
  • The HireYid administrator can access identity uploads strictly for verification and support, under access logging.
  • No one else. Your data is never shared with, sold to, or leaked to other recruiters, advertisers, or third parties — ever.

How it's protected

  • Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Per-recruiter access scoping enforced on the server — an account can only ever query its own applicants.
  • Uploads stored privately with short-lived signed access links, not public URLs.
  • Access to identity documents is logged and audited.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time.
ℹ️ Engineering note (launch stage): The strict access rules above are enforced in HireYid's backend (encrypted storage, server-side authorization, signed URLs, audit logs). This front-end alone cannot guarantee security on its own — the production deployment with that backend is what makes these guarantees binding.
